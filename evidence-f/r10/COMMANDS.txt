Fence under test: admission.RevertEpoch (internal/admission/migrate.go) — the ADR-08 §6a
bad-epoch revert. Dominant cost = the HOLD: every dependent bound to the bad epoch is
fenced fail-closed (epoch_hold audit row + status flip to 'condition'). BUILD-F made the

epoch). Metric = wall-clock latency (ms) of the revert commit that fences them
("time-to-recovered", ADR-08 §6a). perf_budget row: epoch.hold_fence_ms, budget 120 ms,
epoch 1, milestone M6 (registered ADR-13 §3, BUILD-F).
